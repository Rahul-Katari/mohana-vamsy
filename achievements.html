Omega Hospitals is a chain of multi-speciality hospitals in India. The hospital was founded in 2010 by Dr. Ch Mohana Vamsy, a renowned cancer specialist and one of the best oncologists in India.

Dr Vamsy finished his M.B.B.S from one of India's most reputed medical institutes—the Jawaharlal Nehru Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER), Pondicherry. He continued his Master's with a Specialization in General Surgery at JIPMER. With his immense knowledge and passion for finding a cure for pain, Dr. Vamsy continued his education with an M.Ch. (Surg. Oncology) at the Cancer Institute, Adyar, Chennai.​

He was also awarded...​ The Diplomate of National Board (Surgery) from The National Board of Examinations, New Delhi. F.R.C.S. (Surgery) from The Royal College of Surgeons. Edinburgh, The U.K. A Diploma in Laparoscopy Surgery from the University of Strasbourg. France.​

One of the best cancer doctors in India, Dr. Ch Mohana Vamsy, has extensive experience and qualifications in surgical oncology. He aims to make Omega a world leader in healthcare. The hospital aims to provide top-notch medical care and treatment to cancer patients in India by making cancer treatment accessible instead of making them travel long distances to cancer centres.
